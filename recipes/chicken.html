Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Chicken II

Description

Seasoned, browned chicken breasts slow cooked with lemon juice, garlic, and chicken bouilon. A wonderful 'fix and forget' recipe that is easy and pleases just about everyone. Great served with rice or pasta, or even alone.

This recipe is a favorite of mine, as it requires very simple ingredients and very little prep before you throw it in the slow cooker. It's also tasty! With a decent proportion of spices combined with the lemon juice, the chicken has a little extra "kick" to it that I enjoy. I like to make it for my significant other and I when we want a decent meal without having to fuss about the kitchen for half an hour before eating. You can just throw it in the Crock-Pot a few hours ahead of dinnertime, and voila!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Step One: In a bowl, mix the oregano, salt, and pepper. Rub the mixture into the chicken. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat. Brown chicken in butter for 3 to 5 minutes on each side. Place chicken in a slow cooker.
  2. Step Two: In the same skillet, mix the water, lemon juice, garlic, and bouillon. Bring the mixture to boil. Pour over the chicken in the slow cooker.
  3. Step Three: Cover, and cook on High for 3 hours, or Low for 6 hours. Add the parsley to the slow cooker 15 to 30 minutes before the end of the cook time.
