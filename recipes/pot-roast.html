Slow Cooker Beef Pot Roast

Description

The real secret here is making sure you sear the meat before the long, slow braising. Serve with mashed potatoes.

My mother used to make savory, slow cooker pot-roast all the time growing up, and it always brought a smile to my face whenever we had that for dinner. This recipe attempts to replicate that same feeling while also providing a challenge for those looking for it. Though the recipe is more involved than the other recipes, the rewards are worth it.

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 pound) bone-in beef pot roast
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 3 medium carrots
  • 2 stalks celery, cut into chunks
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Step One: Generously season both sides of roast with salt and pepper. Sprinkle flour over the top until well coated, and pat it into the meat. Shake off any excess.
  2. Step Two: Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Sear the roast on both sides for 5-6 minutes each, until well browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Step Three: Reduce the heat to medium and stir in mushrooms and butter; cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Step Four: Stir in onion; cook for 5 minutes, until onions are translucent and begin to brown. Add garlic, stir for about a minute.
  5. Step Five: Stir in 1 1/2 tablespoons flour; cook and stir for about 1 minute. Add tomato paste, and cook for another minute.
  6. Step Six: Slowly add chicken stock, stir to combine, and return to simmer. Remove skillet from the heat.
  7. Step Seven: Place carrots and celery in the slow cooker. Place roast over the vegetables and pour in any accumulated juices. Add rosemary and thyme.
  8. Step Eight: Pour onion and mushroom mixture over the top of the roast. Cover slow cooker, turn to High and cook the roast for 5-6 hours, until the meat is fork tender.
  9. Step Nine: Skim off any fat from the surface and remove the bones. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
